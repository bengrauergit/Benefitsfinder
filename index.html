Step of

📍 Where do you live?

Many benefits are state-specific. Reply with the number for your state or territory.

🎂 How old are you?

Age determines eligibility for pensions, youth allowance, and other programs.

💼 What is your employment status?

This determines which income support payments you may be eligible for.

💵 What is your annual household income?

Income determines eligibility thresholds for most benefits. This is your combined household income before tax.

👨‍👩‍👧 What describes your household?

Family composition affects Family Tax Benefit, Parental Leave, and other family payments.

🏠 What is your housing situation?

Renters may qualify for Rent Assistance, and homeowners may qualify for specific grants.

✅ Do any of these apply to you?

Select all that apply. You can select multiple options. Then click Continue.

🎯 Any other circumstances?

These unlock additional high-value benefits. Select all that apply.
